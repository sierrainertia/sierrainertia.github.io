My story

My Name is Sierra MacDonald. I am a Computer Networking graduate currently working towards a certificate in Technical Writing. I am extremely passionate about how technical writing can help solve business problems and enable users to access different softwares and technology with ease.

In 2018 I graduated from Seneca College in Toronto, Ontario and since then I have been advancing my technical and non-technical skills through school and personal pursuits. Including working on this website to learn more about web design.

I believe that my experience with technology, alongside my experience with technical writing give me the unique ability to take complex technical information and relay it in a simple, user-friendly, and readable way.
